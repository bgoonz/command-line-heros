Automate security and HIPAA compliance

Collecting and interpreting patient data is critical for healthcare organizations. You must also protect that data and comply with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and other standards.

With Red Hat® Ansible® Automation Platform, Red Hat Satellite, and a National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)-certified HIPAA-specific profile from OpenSCAP, you can proactively prevent, detect, and mitigate security threats.

Intelligent Data-as-a-Service for modern data management

To improve the quality of patient care, healthcare organizations need access to the right data at the right time and in the right context.

Eliminate data silos and disparate systems with an architecture that can process data from many sources and intelligently route information to a variety of destinations. Red Hat’s intelligent Data-as-a-Service (iDaaS) solution helps make healthcare data available where it’s needed―in real-time.

Use health data to drive decisions with open source

As the industry rapidly evolves, there is no one-size-fits-all strategy for improving patient outcomes and reducing cost. Healthcare organizations need to make full use of integrated, curated, and enhanced data to gain insights required for improved decision making.

Red Hat’s open source solutions offer the flexibility needed to build a platform that meets your requirements—from clinical decision support to patient engagement. You’ll have the power to use business rules, artificial intelligence (AI), and machine learning (ML) to make decisions and execute the best actions in real-time.

Optimize and modernize IT to improve outcomes

Modern healthcare organizations are using data to improve patient and clinical experiences. Getting real-time insights and capabilities at the point of care requires a flexible IT infrastructure. Central to that is cloud―often hybrid or multicloud―and a trusted application platform.

Our enterprise-ready Kubernetes platform, Red Hat OpenShift®, provides the operational consistency and interoperability needed to advance healthcare IT innovation.
