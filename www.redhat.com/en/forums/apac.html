Cloud-native Development Track: Integration - The Missing Link in your Serverless Adoption Strategy

Integration—the missing link in your serverless adoption strategy
Adoption of serverless platforms are fundamentally changing IT architecture and development. While most organizations have already started to dip their toes into serverless waters, these workloads are often isolated from the core systems driving their business. Bringing serverless to the enterprise in a meaningful way requires the use of integration to bridge this critical gap.
In this breakout session, we'll provide an overview of the key serverless use cases requiring integration and explore their accompanying implementation patterns, including:

  • Event source ingress from alternative protocols, including SaaS and event-driven endpoints.
  • Using Kafka-backed channels with Strimzi to provide scalable and reliable event dispatch within your serverless architecture.
  • Instrumenting your serverless control plane with API management capabilities for serverless APIs.
  • Stateful orchestration of fine-grained serverless functions and services to create complete serverless applications.

The session will cover the latest and greatest open source technologies in the serverless space, including: Knative, Istio, Camel-K, Strimzi, Kafka, and 3scale. Live demos will be used to show real use cases in action.

Sukrit Thareja, Senior Solution Architect, PaaS & Application Services, Red Hat
