Command Line Heroes

Hear about the developers, programmers, hackers, geeks, and open source rebels revolutionizing the technology landscape. Podcast host Saron Yitbarek tells the epic true tales of the people who attempt the extraordinary and persevere against all odds.

An original podcast from

How to listen

There are many ways to listen. Hear the show for free on the platform you prefer:

Subscribe

Subscribe to the show using one of the apps listed above, or your favorite podcast app. Search for Command Line Heroes and hit subscribe. You’ll get new episodes in your feed as soon as they’re released.

Download

Browse the backlog of episodes—and save a few for later. Open a podcast app, search for Command Line Heroes, and download your chosen episodes to your device.

Stream

Each episode has its own page at redhat.com/commandlineheroes. Find the episode you want to hear, and hit play on the embedded web player.

About your host

Saron Yitbarek is a developer and the founder of CodeNewbie, the most supportive community of programmers and people learning to code. She hosts 2 other fantastic shows: CodeNewbie Podcast and Base.cs Podcast.
