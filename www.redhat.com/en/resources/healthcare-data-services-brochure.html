Brief

Red Hat data analytics for healthcare

Healthcare organizations use big data analytics to optimize patient services, lower costs, and gain efficiency. Organizations want to eliminate data redundancy, secure data and preserve its integrity, and share it with others. Data services that capture and share information and build data-driven applications are critical to healthcare modernization. An open source Red Hatdata analytics platform helps healthcare organizations improve data storage, store it safely, and help patients faster.
