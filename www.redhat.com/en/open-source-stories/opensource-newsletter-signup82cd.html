Open Source Stories

Follow your curiosity

Open Source Stories, an original series by Red Hat, celebrates the many people who are using open source technologies to solve everyday problems. This multimedia series explores the impact of open source through short films, articles, and experiences that demonstrate how their work is changing our world for the better. Whether they are using open data to preserve habitats, connecting farms and communities with open source technology, or teaching students how to create collaboratively through open principles, these innovators are truly creating out in the open.
